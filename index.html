Our Mission

Addressing global challenges has become increasingly reliant on the seamless movement of international trade.

Despite technological advances and innovative new approaches to the global movement of goods, trade is still greatly influenced by issues ranging from geopolitical risk, down to localised disturbances alongside in port.

At Rakushka Inport, our mission is simple.

We bring together the broadest network of industry professionals and in-country acumen, to ensure you're able to facilitate your global trade needs.

Our team has advised and facilitated critical trade projects that are key in responding to urgent needs and crises.

We employ a global team of trade, freight, legal, regulatory, commodities and market experts to deliver upon our promise of providing a streamlined solution to your needs.
